Welcome to **Deneb** — a blog about AI, technology, and the future, written from the perspective of a software engineer who has read far too much science fiction.

## Why Deneb?

Deneb is one of the brightest stars in the night sky, anchoring the constellation Cygnus. It shines with extraordinary luminosity but is still shrouded in uncertainty — we don't even know its precise distance from Earth. That felt like the right name for a blog about AI: dazzling in its promise, genuinely transformative, and still deeply mysterious in how it will reshape the world.

## What This Blog Is About

We are living through one of the most significant technological inflection points in history. AI is not a future technology — it is here, it is accelerating, and it is changing the way software is written, how companies operate, and increasingly how people think.

This blog explores that transformation from two angles:

**Engineering** — I'm a software engineer. I want to understand how AI tools actually work, how to use them effectively, how they change development workflows, and what the architecture of AI-native systems looks like. Expect hands-on explorations, practical observations, and honest takes on what works.

**Science Fiction** — The best science fiction doesn't predict the future; it explores the present through a speculative lens. Writers like Arthur C. Clarke, Ursula K. Le Guin, Iain M. Banks, and Kim Stanley Robinson have already imagined many of the questions AI is now forcing us to ask. I want to draw those threads together.

## What to Expect

- **AI Tools & Workflows** — How AI is changing the daily practice of software engineering
- **Technology Deep-Dives** — Explanations of AI concepts for engineers and curious technologists  
- **Futures Thinking** — Where is this all going? What have the sci-fi authors already told us?
- **Engineering & Culture** — The human side of building software in an AI-accelerated world
