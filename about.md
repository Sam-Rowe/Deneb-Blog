      <h1 class="post-title">About Deneb</h1>
    </header>
    <div class="post-content">

<p>
  Deneb is a blog by <strong>Sam Rowe</strong>, a software engineer with a deep curiosity about
  artificial intelligence, emerging technology, and the intersection of engineering pragmatism
  with the boundless imagination of science fiction.
</p>

<p>
  The name <em>Deneb</em> comes from the brightest star in the constellation Cygnus — one of the
  most luminous stars in the Milky Way, yet still largely a mystery. It felt right for a blog
  about a field that is brilliant, vast, and still being understood.
</p>

<h2>What You'll Find Here</h2>

<p>
  This blog explores AI and technology through two lenses:
</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Engineering</strong> — hands-on, practical deep-dives into AI tools, development
      workflows, software architecture, and how AI is changing the craft of building software.</li>
  <li><strong>Science Fiction</strong> — speculation, futures thinking, and how today's technology
      echoes the visions of the sci-fi authors who imagined it decades ago.</li>
</ul>

<p>
  Where those two worlds collide is where Deneb lives.
</p>
